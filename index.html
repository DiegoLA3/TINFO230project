A Very Good Crypto Trading Bot

What is this?

In short, A Very Good Crypto Trading Bot (AVGCTB) is an app that aims to help cryptocurrency investors. The goal is to help you, the investor, to make smart investment decisions and earn more money.

How does it work?

As the name implies, A Very Good Crypto Trading Bot uses a crypto trading bot to trade cryptocurrency. Investors can connect their cryptocurrency exchange accounts and the bot will trade on their behalf.

With the app, you will be able to login with your coinbase and/or behance account. If you happen to be a new investor, you will be able to sign up with either coinbase or behance (or both) from within the app.


The websites that the crypto trading bot uses to get information range from social media sites like Twitter to various news sites. The information gained from these sites is then fed into a sentiment analysis engine.


The bot will use the results it recieves from the sentiment analysis engine to determine the best possible trades for you, along with when the best time to buy or sell is so that you get the best possible outcome from your trading.
